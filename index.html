Who are we?

Hello there! We are Perfection, and we breathe to improve the worklife for global content editors! Our philosophy is that managaging frontends requires more flexibility within the constraints of what the digital eco system allows. We work in the space between digital designers, marketeers and techies allowing them to work in their own tools to offer the best shared result.

We hope that you will follow us as we allow editors to tailor their frontends to perfection!

Who are behind?

The idea behind Perfection was founded by Lars Dyrelund, Steven Singh and Mads Stoumann. Steven has a long background in digital consulting and architecture for enterprise clients and Mads is a very experienced frontend developer. Lars is an experienced product manager with long experience in the space of technology leadership.

What are we doing?

Well, the topic is around digital experiences and our ideas circle around the philosophy to ease the work for content editors. However, we can be more specific.

  • We want to offer a better way to build and collect forms to gather data from Data Subjects in a global context. We want to do this with high focus on compliance and ensure that all stakeholders (editors, developers, compliance and end-users) enjoy our platform (read more here)!>
  • We want to offer Content Editors a simpler way to adjust the look and feel of their components across breakpoints in a simple, intuitiv and visual way. This will increase flexiblity, lower time to market and reduce development cost of trivial variants of visual representation!

Our aim is to become a certified member of The MACH Alliance (Microservice, API-first, Cloud-native, Headless) and integrate in the eco-system as well as participate in the customer care program.
